Based Biscuits

How to make Southern (American) Style Biscuits ... super based.

Summary

This recipe has no short-hand summary

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp of white sugar
  • 1 tbsp of baking powder
  • 8 tbsp of sweet butter
  • 3/4 cup of whole milk (not that 2% trash, get some *REAL* milk)

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 425Â°F
  2. Combine dry ingredients in a bowl
  3. Cube the butter and add it to the dry ingredients
  4. Mix the butter with your hands or a fork until a cornmeal consistency is achieved
  5. Add milk and mix until homogeneous
  6. Roll biscuits out to approximately 3/4 of an inch thick
  7. Use a mug, cup, or cookie cutter to cut out rounds of dough
  8. Arrange rounds of dough in a cast iron pan
  9. Bake for 12-15 minutes
  10. Remove from the oven and eat when hot
  11. Keep the biscuits on the counter or in a brisker
